Writing Project

Moving onto the writing project. This proposal is based on dress coding at school. I wrote a proposal about dress coding because I wanted individuals to become aware of how dress-coding children can become harmful to their mental health. I also added what it's like for the parents of the children to obtain proper clothing for their children to wear at school. I was interested in the dress coding topic because as a kid I saw parents who couldn't buy proper clothing by school standards because they were expensive. That is why I wanted to shed some light on this topic.
